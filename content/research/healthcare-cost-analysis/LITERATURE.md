## Books

### America's Bitter Pill (Steven Brill, 2015)
**Key themes**:
- Hospital "chargemaster" pricing with no market constraints
- Pharmaceutical and device industry lobbying
- Insurance industry complexity and overhead
- Fee-for-service incentivizing volume over value
- ACA creation and implementation

**Relevance**: Provides historical context for cost inflation and identifies systemic drivers of excess spending.
