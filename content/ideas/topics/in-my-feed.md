## The Quick Download

Less original writing, more signal. This is a running catalog of podcasts, videos, and articles I'm finding genuinely useful — the things that are shaping how I think about AI, healthcare, and building software. If I'm posting it here, it changed something about how I see the problem.

## Why I Keep This

The discourse around AI moves fast and most of it is noise. I want a record of what actually moved the needle for me — not what was trending, but what was clarifying. Looking back through this kind of log is how I track how my thinking has changed.

## What Makes the Cut

I'm not trying to build an exhaustive reading list. If it's here, it's because:

- It explained something I'd been fuzzy on in a way that actually stuck
- It introduced a framing I hadn't seen before
- It said something true that most people aren't saying yet

Most of what I consume doesn't make it here. The stuff that does is worth your time.

## The Format

Each entry is short — podcast or source, what it's about, and the one thing that was worth the listen or read. No essays. Just the signal.
