## The Quick Download

Not everything fits in a category. This is the place for observations, half-formed opinions, and things that stuck with me but don't belong anywhere else. Some of it is about AI. Some of it isn't. None of it is trying to be a take.

## What This Is

I spend a lot of time on the water — kayaking, mostly, but also just sitting near it. Something about that kind of slow, open attention makes patterns obvious that aren't visible when you're moving fast. This section is named after that feeling.

The posts here tend to be shorter and more direct than the other categories. An observation I made, something I read that shifted how I think about a problem, a question I don't have a good answer to yet. Local Chief (my voice capture setup) is where most of these start — an idea said out loud, then written up.

## The Vibe

Less analysis, more noticing. I'm not trying to have a hot take on every AI development. I'm trying to pay attention to what's actually happening and write about the things that seem important but aren't obvious yet.

## What You'll Find

A mix of things. Some posts are about AI and tech. Some are about how I think about my work. Some are just things I noticed that seemed worth writing down. If the other sections feel too structured, start here.
